ABOUT US

JustRight is a platform that empowers individuals to report crimes and injustices they have faced or witnessed. Our mission is to bridge the gap between victims and justice by providing a secure and supportive environment.

Moderators, including legal experts and advocates, are available to offer guidance and support, ensuring that every report is taken seriously and acted upon. We believe in the power of community and the importance of standing up for what is right.

Join us in our mission to create a safer, fairer society, one report at a time.
